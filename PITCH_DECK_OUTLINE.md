## PRESENTATION TIPS

### Timing (5 minutes total)
- Slide 1: 15 seconds (hook)
- Slide 2: 30 seconds (problem)
- Slide 3: 30 seconds (insight)
- Slide 4: 30 seconds (solution)
- Slide 5: 90 seconds (demo - MOST IMPORTANT)
- Slide 6: 30 seconds (platform effect)
- Slide 7: 30 seconds (business model)
- Slide 8: 30 seconds (competitive advantage)
- Slide 9: 30 seconds (trust & ethics)
- Slide 10: 30 seconds (vision & ask)

### Delivery
- Start with energy (hook them immediately)
- Make eye contact with judges
- Use hand gestures (natural, not excessive)
- Vary vocal tone (emphasize key points)
- Pause for impact (after big statements)
- Smile (show passion and confidence)

### Demo Best Practices
- Practice 10+ times (know it cold)
- Have backup video (in case of technical issues)
- Narrate what's happening (don't assume they see it)
- Highlight "wow" moments (speed, explainability)
- Keep it simple (don't show everything)

### Q&A Preparation
- Anticipate questions (see HACKATHON_BLUEPRINT.md)
- Answer confidently (even if you don't know, pivot)
- Use appendix slides (have them ready)
- Stay calm (judges are testing you)
- End with enthusiasm (leave them excited)
